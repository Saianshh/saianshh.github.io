Philosophy

My reasoning for being a computer scientist is because I see the future of our world in technology. It has always been a dream of mine to create and be a part of something that can change the world for the better, and problem solving as well as having the tools to create software brings me one step closer to that goal. Computer Science gives you the tools needed to redefine the future. I've always loved the idea of being able to make a website that millions of people can use, or an app that the world can benefit from. Throughout my undergraduate and graduate degrees, I've been learning these tools and have been inching closer to that dream. In doing my classes, it's less of a chore and more of a passion, making it all the more easier to work hard to do well. I've always had a philosophy of never chasing success, but rather chasing excellence. I truly believe that if you work to be the best there is and best you possibly can be at whatever you want to do, the success will come on its own.
